Junior fullstack developer & UX / UI Designer

I’m Julia, a software developer living in Tartu, Estonia. My coding journey began in 2020 spring. I have previous experience with Graphic Design for several years making advertisements and magazines. My coding interest started with searching through web for design inspirations. Seeing web developer's works made me really envy! I was excited seeing attractive dynamic homepages and started to train on Freecodecamp.

Feeling curious and bored at the same time solving small parts of code moved me to Youtube tutorials and then decided to enroll to advanced course in Tartu Vocational Education Centre.
In my free time I am working to improve my front-end and back-end skills.

Love that now I can combine Design and Web Development. I want to develop my back-end skills. Thats why I want to find place for internship.
